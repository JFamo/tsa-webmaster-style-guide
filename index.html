Style Guide?

A style guide helps create a consistent Brand Image.

This style guide was created for Boyertown TSA's 2018-19 webmaster team, based on the original style guide from RIT's BTC club by Ethan Witherington.

Brand Ethos

Purpose

Chapter Resource and Public-Facing Promotion

Our website will assist chapter members while also promoting the chapter to the general public.

Content will include chapter fundraisers, activities, officers, history, motto, creed, logo, images of chapter functions, past submissions, a competitive record, and more.

This site will also include our CTE, with a listing and details of STEM courses at the school. We must include teacher bios. We need to find a way to effectively organize this abundance of information.

Rubric

The site is worth 130/200 points, which is significant. Of that, 100/130 are related to the chapter and CTE half of the site.

Our target audience requires a specific tone of voice to have the maximum impact. For more information, see the Tone and Voice section.

The rubric includes the following sections

  • Ease of Layout and Navigation (20)
  • Well-used Graphics and an Attractive Color Scheme (20)
  • Usability on most Devices and Browsers; No Broken Links (20)
  • Spelling and Grammar (10)
  • CTE Content (20)
  • Chapter Content (20)

Logo, Brandname

Logo

Our logo forever is the TSA logo in white, without any text in top or bottom.

It's a PNG file so it's transparent. I don't think we need it anywhere other than the top.

Appropriate backgrounds include Dark, DarkGray, and Base. Accent and lighter colors provide too little contrast. See the Color Section for more..

Brandname

Long (Official) Form:

Boyertown Area Senior High School Technology Student Association
  • First letter of each word is capitalized
  • We may not use this anywhere on the actual site
  • Stand-Alone, no other words should follow it.
  • Roboto Bold

Short (Informal) Form:

BASH TSA
  • Space, no hyphen
  • Roboto Bold
  • May be followed by other specifiers
  • For Example:
BASH TSA Style-Guide BASH TSA Wiki

Color Palette

Dark: #1e1e1e

DarkGray: #43494D

Gray: #E9EBED

LightGray: #F5F5F5

Light: #FBFBFB

Base: #3e92cc

Hilight: #3E92CC

Accent: #D62828

The main color of the logo was extracted as the Accent color. From there, Base was chosen such that Accent would complement it.

When using these colors, ensure there is enough contrast between backgrounds and text. LightGray is typically the best background, as there are both lighter and darker colors to complement it.

Use of space

1rem margins should be everywhere. Items in boxes should also employ 1rem padding.
